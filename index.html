Things you need to know

  • To play, wait until a fumo shows up, then bonk it by moving your pointer over it and pressing Z. If you're using a phone, just tap on the fumo.
  • Keep bonking fumos! The game speed starts at 1000ms. Every 10 hits, you go to the next level, and the speed gets faster by decreasing 50ms. This continues until the speed reaches a maximum of 150ms.
  • Your life bar goes down by 5% when a fumo appears, and goes up by 5% when you bonk one. The game ends when your life bar is empty.
